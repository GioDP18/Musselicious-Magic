Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open from 9:00 am to 5:00pm on Weekdays.

Do you source your seafood sustainably?

Yes, we prioritize sustainable sourcing and work with trusted suppliers who follow ethical fishing practices.

Are there options for customers with shellfish or other seafood allergies?

Absolutely! We have a variety of dishes that cater to different dietary needs, including options for those with shellfish or seafood allergies.

Are there non-seafood options available for individuals who do not consume seafood?

Absolutely! We understand that not everyone consumes seafood, and we have a range of non-seafood options available to cater to different dietary preferences.

Are there any student-led initiatives or programs related to seafood sustainability or aquaculture?

Yes, we are proud to support various student-led initiatives and programs focused on seafood sustainability and aquaculture. We actively collaborate with local schools and organizations to promote responsible seafood practices.

Experience the taste of the ocean with our Tahong Spring Roll, where plump mussels are delicately wrapped in crispy spring roll pastry and fried to golden perfection.
